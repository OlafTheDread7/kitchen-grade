About Kitchen Grade

Kitchen Grade is an independent kitchen-gear review site. We research the best air fryers, espresso machines, blenders, chef's knives, and cookware so you can spend your money on tools that earn their counter space. How We Test

Our recommendations combine hands-on evaluation with broad research. For each category we:

  • Compare expert reviews and professional test-kitchen results across multiple trusted sources.
  • Analyze thousands of verified owner reviews to surface real-world reliability and common complaints.
  • Rank every pick head-to-head against direct competitors at the same price point.
  • Prioritize cooking performance, build quality, ease of cleaning, and long-term durability over flashy specs.
  • Revisit and update our picks as new models launch and prices shift.
